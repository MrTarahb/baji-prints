About.

I make photographs and print them. There's a gap between an image on a screen and an image on paper — in the weight of it, the texture, the light it holds. My work lives in that gap.

I work across ICM, abstract, street, and macro. Most of what I make is in black and white, though colour finds its way in when it earns it.

Based in Wiedikon, Zürich. I work from a small atelier with a fully colour-calibrated setup on an Epson SC-P900 — up to A2, across a range of fine art papers.

Epson SC-P900 · 10-channel pigment ink · Up to A2 · Photo Rag Pearl · Mono Silk Warmtone · Photo Rag Satin & more
